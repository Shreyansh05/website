Machu Picchu

Machu Picchu was built around the 15th century in the mountains of Peru. This remarkable site is renowned for its breathtaking mountainous setting and intricate stone structures. It is designated as a UNESCO World Heritage Site, and it stands as one of the most important archaeological wonders, offering insights into the advanced civilization of the Incas.
